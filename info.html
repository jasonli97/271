Solutions for a connected way to manage housing and student credentials

WelcomeU offers a solution to allow students connect with other peers, manage housing, and use a compatiable device to unlock their dorm rooms. This way students don't have an issue when finding roomates, chatting with classmates and being locked out their dorm room.

We stand out from the rest since we offer a more unified approach. Instead of having mutiple sites or apps to download. Students are able to access our servies on desktop and mobile. From just one site.

This also offers University admins in having a unified approach to manage student credentials, and housing . We also intergrate with existing SSO credentials for an easier sign in process.
